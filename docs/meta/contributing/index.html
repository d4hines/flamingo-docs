Contributing ğŸ™Œ

The Flamingo roadmap is available on Github!

There are lots of fun, hard problems to solve around the language itself, but the best way to contribute at the moment is to think of use cases! Flamingo enables a new kind of rapid prototyping combined with formal verification, and that's novel combination - one that might even seem like an oxymoron at first. If you come up with a super cool use case, post it as an issue on Github!
